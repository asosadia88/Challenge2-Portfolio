About Me

My name is Annielys Sosa. I was born in Habana, Cuba and emigrated to the United States with my parents, via the lottery of Visas at the age of 7.

I earned my Bachelor in Microbiology and Cell Science from the University of Florida and Master in Finance from the University of Miami.



I have proudly served as the Director of Student Life at Miami Dade College, Wolfson Campus for nine years. I am also a current UM Coding BootCamp student learning numerous marketable technologies, including JavaScript, Node, SQL, MongoDB, React and more!
